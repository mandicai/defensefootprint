Welcome to Defense Footprint.

Where is the US military? For most civilians, it can be very difficult to wrap our minds around everything the US military does around the world. The military doesn’t just wage war; from advise and assist to humanitarian aid and everything in between, our American troops are engaged in missions in almost every nation worldwide.

Defense Footprint’s mission is to educate the public on the U.S.’s global military presence by visualizing the factors contributing to its “defense footprint”.
