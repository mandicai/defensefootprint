About

Our mission is twofold.

  • Provide plain speak/nonpartisan educational materials for the public, because the US military’s activities are vast and varied. It is very daunting for the uninitiated to get a comprehensive understanding of what the US military is doing overseas, and we want to provide a comprehensive resource for the public to that end.
  • Provide comprehensive, regularly updated, and easily accessible datasets for researchers to use.
