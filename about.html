About

Our mission

Defense Footprint is a non-partisan project that promotes a stronger public understanding of the US military. We are neither watchdog activists nor a pro-military group; instead, we present relevant statistics in an accessible way. We believe that Americans should be informed about what their largest public institution does—as a civilian-led military, the US military is the responsibility of the American people. We call this need to be informed “defense literacy.” Because statistics on the military’s activities differ depending on the data source, our approach is to offer these different sources side-by-side and let people form their own opinions. We hope that increasing defense literacy will empower ordinary Americans to take informed stances on military issues—be they hawkish or dovish, liberal or conservative—and to constructively debate them in the public sphere.
